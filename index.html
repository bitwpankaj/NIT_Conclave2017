About NIT CONCLAVE


“For good ideas and true innovation, we need human interaction, conflict, arguments and debate”. All of us, at some point or another must have thought some new, innovative ideas to solve existing problems or improvise the techniques to handle it. But how many of us can claim to have developed the thought into something tangible? The reason our ideas die before witnessing the real world is because either we don’t pursue them as passionately as we should or we don’t get a platform to discuss it. The NIT Conclave is an annual meeting of students from all National Institutes of Technology which provides a common platform for the discussion and implementation of innovative ideas. It proposes great prospects of discussion on Technology, Entrepreneurship, Research, Training and Placements. It targets the participation and contribution of the new species of optimistic & ambitious young engineers, the future harbingers of progress for our country to build base for such a symbiotic relationship amongst NITs that can sustain itself for the years to come and contribute to the greater cause of nation building An aspect of this event is the effective exchange of knowledge, ideas and resource base to bring all the NITs at a uniform level of development, upholding the very concept of inclusive growth - the primary strategy for genuine progress of a nation. It aims to present the unique initiatives taken by various NITs to in order to produce assets for the nation. The NIT Conclave believes that innovative strategies, creativity and cumulative strength are necessary for the upliftment of a country.

NIT Silchar takes a great pleasure while informing that we are hosting the next edition i.e, 7th NIT Conclave. Since 2011, the NIT Conclave is providing the aspiring entrepreneurs with a promoting platform to give them a head start. The correct patronage can help to produce challenging future industrialists>and a strong industrial base will eventually contribute in the growth of the Indian economy. The theme being " OUTCOME BASED RESEARCH, INNOVATION and ENTREPRENUERSHIP " is an attempt to encourage the innovative ideas to bloom and thrive. Introducing the concept of ‘Outcome based Research’, we take an opportunity to captivate the attention of young minds who have some thrilling research oriented ideas persisting within their brains. ‘Innovation’ embeds the deliberate application of imagination, information and initiatives in deriving the best out of available resources.
